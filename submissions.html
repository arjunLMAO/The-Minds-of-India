III. Submissions
Write
to us.

Dear Writer,

In an age where the media we consume is plagued by curated narratives, authentic voices, particularly the youth's voices, can struggle to find space and are often shut down. We believe that the media should be a reflection of the youth's raw energy and unadulterated truth. We bring you a platform where ideas are expressed articulately, honestly, and deeply.

With this vision in mind, we at The Minds of India invite young writers, photographers, and creators to contribute to our monthly newsletter.

If you wish to have any of your work published, please send your submission to themindsofindia1@gmail.com.

Regards,
The Minds of India Team

Send your work tothemindsofindia1@gmail.com
Before you send

Submission notes.

  1. Please attach written submissions as a Word document (.doc or .docx).
  2. Use a clear file name with your name and the title of your work.
  3. Include your name and a short note introducing your submission in the email.
  4. For photographs or other visual work, include high-resolution files and any relevant captions.

We look forward to reading you.
